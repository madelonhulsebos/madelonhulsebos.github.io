--- layout: page title: Table Representation Learning Lab permalink: /trl-lab/ --- Welcome 👋!

The mission of the TRL Lab (short for Table Representation Learning lab) is to democratize insights from structured data, through representation learning and generative models for tabular data. The lab is led by Madelon Hulsebos who also initiated the broader Table Representation Learning theme at ELLIS Amsterdam.
