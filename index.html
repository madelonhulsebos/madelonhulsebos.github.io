Welcome

Hi, I am Madelon Hulsebos, a PhD student at the University of Amsterdam and intern at Sigma Computing.

Throughout the years, I received many messages about organizations that struggle to leverage their databases due to unstandardized and lacking semantic metadata. I also observed this myself as a data scientist. In 2018, at the MIT Media Lab, I got the opportunity to contribute a method (Sherlock) aimed at capturing the column semantics of tables at scale to tackle this problem.

Stimulated by the rapid advances in deep learning, I believe accurately recovering semantics from tabular data in practice is within close reach. I contribute to this as a PhD student at the INDE Lab at the University of Amsterdam advised by Prof. Paul Groth and co-advised by Çağatay Demiralp (industry).

Feel welcome to reach out!
