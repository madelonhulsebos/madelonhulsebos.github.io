Welcome

Hi, I am Madelon Hulsebos. I am a PhD candidate at the University of Amsterdam and a Research Intern at Sigma Computing.

Throughout the years, I heard many stories about organizations that struggle to unlock the value in their databases due to unaligned data source systems and lacking semantic metadata. I also observed it myself. In 2018, at the MIT Media Lab, I got the chance to contribute the method (Sherlock) to tackle semantic type detection for tabular data at scale, which is meant to partly solve this issue.

Stimulated by the rapid advances in the field of natural language processing, I believe accurately recovering semantics from tabular data is within close reach. I contribute to this myself as a PhD candidate at the INDE Lab at the University of Amsterdam advised by Paul Groth and co-advised by Çağatay Demiralp.

Feel free to reach out!
