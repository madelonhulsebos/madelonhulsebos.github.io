Welcome

Hi, I am Madelon Hulsebos, a PhD student at the University of Amsterdam and research intern at Sigma Computing.

Throughout the years, I received many messages about organizations that struggle to leverage their databases due to unaligned data source systems and/or lacking semantic metadata. I also observed it myself as a data scientist. In 2018, at the MIT Media Lab, I got the opportunity to contribute a method, (Sherlock), aimed at capturing the column semantics of tables at scale to partially tackle this problem.

Stimulated by the rapid advances in deep learning and natural language processing, I believe accurately recovering semantics from tabular data in practice is within close reach. I contribute to this as a PhD student at the INDE Lab at the University of Amsterdam advised by Prof. Paul Groth and co-advised by Çağatay Demiralp from Sigma Computing.

Feel welcome to reach out!
