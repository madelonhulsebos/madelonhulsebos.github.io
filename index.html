Welcome

Hi, I am Madelon Hulsebos. I have been a data scientist since 2019, where I currently work for Heineken.

Throughout the last few years, I heard many stories about how organisations struggle to unlock the value in their data due to unaligned data source systems. I also observed it myself. In 2018 at the MIT Media Lab, I got the chance to contribute a method (Sherlock) to tackle semantic type detection for tabular data which is meant to partly solve this issue.

With the rapid developments in the field of NLP, I believe there is a lot to gain in this area for the upcoming years. Actually, I will contribute to this myself, as I will be a PhD candidate at the INDE Lab Lab of the University of Amsterdam supervised by Paul Groth with Çağatay Demiralp as my co-supervisor. During my PhD I will continue my research in recovering semantics from tabular data.

For more background, you can find my CV here, check out the Projects and Posts pages, or check out my other pages (see below).
